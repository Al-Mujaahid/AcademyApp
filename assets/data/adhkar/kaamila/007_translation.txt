YAA ‘AAFI BIL IHSAANI WAL IKHTIYAARI ANID DURRI WAT TU’ASAAI
WA YAA MU’AAFI BIL JABAARUUTI WAS SULTAANI ‘ANIS SUU-I BIL WAFAAI
ANTAL MUWAFFIQU BIL ‘ATHAMATI ‘ALAA ITHBATIL IKHLAASI WAL ADAAI
WA ANTAL MUTA’AALI ‘ALAL JAMEE’I BIL MALAAKUUTI WAL KIBRIYAAI
ANTAN NASEERU BIL AAYAATI HEENA WAKAYFA TASHAAU ‘ALAL A’ADAAI
WA ANTAL MU’IZZU WAL MUDHILLU ‘ALAL BARAAYA BIL QUDRATI WAL QADAAI
YAA KHAAFIDU YAA RAAFI’U ANTAL MUGHNI BIR RUSHDI WAL I’TIDAAI
YAA WALIYYU YAA JALEELU ANTAL KAREEMU BIL ISLAAMI WAN NA’MAAI
YAA HAMEEDU YAA MAJEEDU ANTAL GHANIYYU BIL ’IZZI WAL JUUDI ‘ANI AGHNIYAAI
YAA MUQSITU YAA MUQEETU ANTAR RAHMAANU BIS SAWAABIGHI LIKAAFFATIR RUHAMAAI
BINAL FAQRU WAD DURRU WAL HUZNU WAL HAMMU WAL KARBU WAS SUQAMAAI
WA BIKAL GHINAA WAL ‘AFWU WAL ‘AAFIYATU WAS SALAAMU WAL BARAKATU WASH SHIFAAU
ISRIF ‘ANNAA YAA LATEEFUL WAHALA WAL FASHALA WASH SHAQAAWATA WAL BA-SAAU
WA LAA TUSHMIT BINAA YAA MUGHEETHUL MUTAKABBIREENAL HAASIDEENAL ASHQIYAAI
YAA QAADHIFU BIL HAQQI ‘ALAL BAATILI WA YAA MUQMI’ASH SHAYAATEENIR RUJAMAAI
NAJJINAA MINAL WASWASATI WA SIHRI WAMAKRIL MAAKIREENAL AKHILLAAI
WA MIN DALAALATIL HAWA WA SHUHHIN NAFSI WA DEEQIL ‘AYSHI WA TAMA’IL JUHALAAI
WA HAZZIZ LANAA QAWAA-IDAL KUFRI WAL GHULUUWI WAT TARAFI WAL I’TIDAAI
YAA MUSHATTITA SHAMLIL FUJJAARI WAL FUSSAAQI WAL MUSHRIKEENAL ADHILLAAI
WA YAA MUMAZZIQA AHZAABIT TAWAAGHEETI WA JUNUUDI IBLEESAL KHUBATHAAI
YAA MUNTAQIMUH ZIM LANAA ZAHFAHUM WA BADDID KUTLATAHUM MUNQALIBEENA JUBANAAI
WAFARRIQ JAM’AHUM WASHALLIL ‘AZMAHUM MUKHDHUULEENA BIJUNUUDIKAL ASHIDDAAI
YAA MUBTILA KAYDIL KAA-IDEENA WA YAA HAFEETHADH DHIKRI WAL ANBIYAAI
YAA MUJRIYAS SAHAABI WAL AMTAARI WA YAA MANNAANU BIHUSNIL ASMAAI
YAA MAN ‘ALAL ‘ARSHIL ‘ATHEEMI STAWAA HANNAANAL ARDI WAS SAMAAI
YAA MUSAKHKHIRAR REEHIL ‘AASIFATI WA YAA MUDABBIRA UMUURIL WUKALAAI
YAA DHAL HABLISH SHADEEDI UKTUB LANAA INDAKA MINAL MUJAAHIDEENAL KHULAFAAI
WA YAA DHAL AMRIR RASHEEDI AKRIMNAA BIL IJAABATI MINAS SU’ADAAI
YAA MAN AORATHAL ARDA LIS SAALIHEENAS SALAFI WAR RASHIDEENAL KHULAFAAI
WA SADAQA WA’DAHUL MURSALEENA WAR RIBBIYYEENA BIL BURHAANI WAL I’TILAAI
YAA MUAZZIZA RUSULIHI WAL MU’MINEENAL MUQAATILEENA ‘AIZZAAI
YAA MUAYYIDAL HAQQI BIL QU’RAANI WAN NUURI WAL MALAAIKATIL KURAMAAI
IKHTIM LANAA BIN NASRI WAL FAOZI WAJ’ALNAA MINAL ‘AAMILEENAL ‘ULAMAAI
WA ADKHILNAA BIRAHMATIKA YAOMAL QIYAAMATI MA’AL AIMMATISH SHUHADAAI
ANTAL ‘ALEEMU BIL KHAFAAYA WA ANTA RAHEEMUL MALHUUFI WAD DU’AFAAI
ANTAL QAHHAARU FAOQA ‘IBAADIHI WA ANTAL ‘ALIYYU ‘ANISH SHURAKAAI
ANTA SHAKUURUSH SHAAKIREENA WA ANTA ILAAHUL ‘AABIDEENAL QUDAMAAI
ANTAL MUHEETU BIL ‘AALAMEENA WA RAUUFUS SAABIQEENAL ATQIYAAI
YAA MAN BIYADIHI NAWAASIYATUL INSI WAL JINNI MINAL AMWAATI WAL AHYAAI
